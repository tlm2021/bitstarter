Try as they might, no modern video games can fully capture the excitement and freedom of a classic pen-and-paper RPG. But in this day and age, why do we need the pen-and-paper? With the wealth of content available for your traditional RPG, there has to be a better way to discover, sort, and manage that content wich lets you focus on building your worlds, telling your stories, and figuring out what mechanic to use to allow your friend to bang that wench he sees in the bar instead of listenting to King's messenger talk about some old wizard who's causing trouble.

You're friends should be limited by your geography. Create gaming sessions that anyone in the world can join. Play with people you know, or open your game up to the entire world! Whatever session you join, you have access to all of their content, allowing you to try out new things. Dive into dungeons, climb castles, rescue fair maidens, and ultimately get killed because you couldn't resist slapping your mate on the back while he was playing that knife game. But most of all, play your game, in your world, your way.

FAQ

What is Virtual Basement Games?
Virtual Basement Games is a company dedicated to bringing classic 20th century gaming experiences into the modern era without sacrificing the dynamic, open-ended and social aspects of those games that allowed them to flurish.
Don't MMOs do that?
Not even close. MMOs do offer very rich worlds with a ton of variety. And some are even now allowing user generated content. But these are still someone else's worlds, and you're playing by someone else's rules. Part of what makes pen-and-paper RPGs great is their adaptability and flexibilty to what YOU want to do. Tell your story, the way you want to tell it. And have the real ability to have an impact in that world.
So how do I get content?
Initially, manual entry. You enter information about equipment, monsters, characters, items and more, and we save it to your account and make it available to all players in a play session. In the future, we hope to make available official content from the biggest names in RPG publishing for you to browse, buy, and have for all your future game sessions. With slick integration of content throughout the interface, beautiful illustrations, and access to it in any game you participate in.
